Lego The Avengers

An entirely new world

S.H.I.E.L.D. has located the mysterious Tesseract device and the Army's super soldier Captain America.

The Tesseract is actually a gateway to an entirely new world called Asgard.

A mysterious being known as Loki arrives on earth and immediately assumes that he can rule all human beings.

But that irks S.H.I.E.L.D. director Nick Fury the wrong way. As Loki escapes with the Tesseract, Nick Fury believes this is an act of war against Earth.
